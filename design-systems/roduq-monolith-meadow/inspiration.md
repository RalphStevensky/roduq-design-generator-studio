## Typography references

## Polish character handling

Serif fonts dla display — verify diacritics render OK (ą/ć/ę/ł/ń/ó/ś/ź/ż) w wybranym font. Recoleta + Fraunces both support full Polish charset.

## Anti-patterns

❌ **Generic green** — sage specific (#5C7A4F), NIE bright green
❌ **Photo-as-decoration** — photos must serve narrative
❌ **Sans-serif display** — defeats heritage atmosphere
✅ **Warm cream surfaces** (#FAFAF7) NIE pure white
✅ **Long-form text** allowed — narrative > brevity for hero
✅ **Hand-crafted feel** — irregular padding OK
