# Roduq Monolith Meadow — Warm Earthy

> Category: Roduq Heritage / Hospitality / Wellness
> Tagline: "Solidność z natury. Idealne dla heritage / hospitality / wellness."

## Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Warm, earthy, place-based. Sage green + warm terracotta + cream surfaces. Inspired by Polish heritage architecture (twierdza-boyen sanitized) + Scandinavian hospitality + Patagonia storytelling.

Single accent: sage green (#5C7A4F). Terracotta (#C97B5A) appears as semantic-accent for secondary CTAs — counts towards 2-uses-per-screen cap.

## Color Palette

### Primary
- **Background** (`#FAFAF7`): cream canvas — never pure white
- **Surface** (`#FFFFFF`): raised cards/modals
- **Foreground** (`#1A1C19`): primary text — never pure black
- **Accent — Sage** (`#5C7A4F`): primary CTAs, links, brand
- **Terracotta** (`#C97B5A`): warm secondary accent (used sparingly)

### Neutrals & Borders
- Muted: `#525249`
- Meta: `#8A8A7F`
- Border: `rgba(26, 28, 25, 0.14)`
- Border soft: `rgba(26, 28, 25, 0.08)`

## Typography

- **Display**: `Recoleta`, `Fraunces`, Georgia, serif — humanist z personality
- **Body**: `Inter`, system-ui, sans-serif — readability priority
- **Mono**: rare use

Scale: standard Roduq scale. Larger line-height for long-form (1.65-1.75).

## Voice & Tone

- Warm, storytelling, place-based
- Use sensory language ("feel the stone walls", "morning light")
- Long-form copy OK — historical context, narrative
- Polish translation thoughtful — preserve cultural nuance

## Layout Principles

- Asymmetric hero z large featured image
- Photo galleries z masonry layout
- Long-form sections z generous line-height (1.7+)
- Texture overlays subtle (noise / paper grain — optional)
- Buttons z rounded corners (radius-lg)
