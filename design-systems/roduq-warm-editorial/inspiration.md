# Roduq Warm Editorial — Inspiration

## Gold standard references

### [The New York Times](https://nytimes.com)
- Serif body + display
- Single-column article view, 65ch max
- Byline + reading time
- Pull quotes z large display typography
- **Co kopiować**: type hierarchy, article-page format

### [The Atlantic](https://theatlantic.com)
- Dropcap first letter
- Generous line-height (1.75)
- Inline images z italic captions
- **Co kopiować**: dropcap pattern, caption typography

### [Substack publications](https://substack.com)
- Author-centric layout
- Newsletter signup prominent
- Simple monetization (subscribe button)
- **Co kopiować**: byline format, subscribe CTA placement

### [Pitchfork](https://pitchfork.com)
- Score-driven editorial reviews
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Music criticism authority
- **Co kopiować**: review header pattern

### [Stripe Press](https://press.stripe.com)
- Book product pages w editorial voice
- Beautiful typography setting
- Long-form excerpts
- **Co kopiować**: book/long-form product page format

## Anti-patterns

❌ **Sans-serif body** — defeats editorial atmosphere
❌ **16px body** — too small dla long reads (use 18-20px)
❌ **Multi-column layout** dla articles — single column 65ch ideal
❌ **Heavy ads / popups** during reading — kills flow
❌ **Skipping caption italics** — gold standard editorial pattern
✅ **Dropcap first letter** w article opens
✅ **Pull quotes prominent** z display typography
✅ **Byline + reading time + date** w header consistently
✅ **Subscribe CTA at article end** (Substack pattern)
