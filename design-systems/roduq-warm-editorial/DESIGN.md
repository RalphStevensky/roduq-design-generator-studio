# Roduq Warm Editorial — Magazine Style

> Category: Roduq Editorial / Long-form / Content
> Tagline: "Słowa mają wagę. Dla content-driven sites — blog, newsletter, journalism."

## Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Considered, long-form, voice-led. Tiempos / Fraunces serif body + headlines. Cream surfaces, brick red accent, forest green secondary. Optimized for reading: 65ch line length, 1.7 line-height, dropcaps.

Best dla: blogs, newsletters, online magazines, content-led marketing sites, niche publications, author landing pages.

## Color Palette

### Primary
- **Background** (`#FFFAF5`): butter cream — never pure white
- **Surface** (`#FFFFFF`): raised cards, modal — used sparingly
- **Foreground** (`#1C1C1C`): primary text — near black z slight warmth
- **Accent — Brick** (`#A8321B`): primary CTAs, links, pull quotes
- **Forest** (`#0E4F35`): secondary accent (used sparingly for variety)

### Neutrals
- Muted: `#5C5C5C`
- Meta: `#8A8A8A`
- Border: `rgba(28, 28, 28, 0.16)`
- Border soft: `rgba(28, 28, 28, 0.08)`

## Typography

- **Display**: `Tiempos Headline` (Klim) lub `Fraunces` (display) — editorial serif
- **Body**: `Tiempos Text` lub `Lora` lub `Source Serif Pro` — readable serif body
- **Mono**: rare use (`JetBrains Mono` gdy needed)

Body settings:
- Font-size 18-20px (larger than typical 16px)
- Line-height 1.7 (relaxed dla long reads)
- Max content width 65ch (optimal reading)
- First-letter dropcap dla articles

## Voice & Tone

- Considered, well-researched, conversational z author voice
- Long paragraphs OK (5+ sentences w right context)
- Subheadings frequent (every 2-3 paragraphs)
- Pull quotes wyróżnione

## Layout Principles

- Single-column reading view (no sidebar dla articles)
- Bylines + estimated reading time
- Inline images z italic captions
- Pull quotes z large display typography
- Footer z "related articles" section
