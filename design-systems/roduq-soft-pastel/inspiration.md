## Anti-patterns

❌ **Sharp corners** — defeats soft feeling (radius >= 1rem everywhere)
❌ **High-contrast colors** — pastels muted, NIE saturated
❌ **Hard shadows** — soft shadows only (high blur, low opacity)
❌ **Aggressive copy** — encouraging > prescriptive
❌ **Emoji overuse** — 1-2 per page max
✅ **Pill buttons** (radius-full)
✅ **Soft illustrations** w pastel colors
✅ **Generous padding** (more than default)
✅ **Avatar groups w testimonials**
✅ **Warm dark fg** (#37352F NIE pure black)
