## Anti-patterns

❌ **Pure #000000 background** — vibrates, eye strain (use #0A0A0A)
❌ **Pure #FFFFFF text** — too harsh (use #FAFAFA)
❌ **Light font weights** (< 400) — hard to read on dark
❌ **High-saturation accent overuse** — even neon needs restraint (max 2 visible accents)
❌ **Heavy glows everywhere** — restrain to 1-2 hero glows
✅ **Backdrop blur navigation** (12px standard)
✅ **Subtle gradients hero** (radial glows at 25-30% positions)
✅ **Border opacity 6-12%** for cards (vs solid borders)
✅ **Cards on hover get subtle glow** (purple/cyan tint)
✅ **Code blocks z dracula / github-dark theme**
