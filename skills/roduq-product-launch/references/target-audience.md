# Product Launch — Target Audience Profiles

## Persona 1: Early Adopter (25-45)

- **Context**: Always trying new products, signs up for waitlists regularly
- **State**: Curious, FOMO-driven
- **Decision speed**: Fast — joins waitlist w seconds gdy interested
- **Needs**:
  - Quick signup (email only)
  - Clear value prop (what is this?)
  - Launch date visible
  - Discount / early access incentive
- **Trust signals**: founder credibility, press mentions, prototype photos
- **Mobile usage**: high (~70% mobile signups)

**Copy strategy**:
- Hero anticipatory + countdown
- Single-field email form
- Counter visible (social proof)
- Incentive clear ("pierwsi 500 — 30% off")
- Founder photo in hero or story

## Persona 2: Skeptical Buyer (30-55)

- **Context**: Burned by Kickstarter failures, careful with money
- **State**: Cautious, evidence-seeking
- **Decision speed**: Slow — 1-4 weeks deliberation
- **Needs**:
  - Detailed FAQ
  - Risk disclosure ("co jeśli opóźnienie?")
  - Refund policy clear
  - Press mentions / external validation
  - Founder background
- **Trust signals**: prior shipping history, press, transparency about challenges
- **Mobile usage**: medium

**Copy strategy**:
- FAQ comprehensive z risk disclosure
- "Updates" section showing development progress
- Founder credentials prominent
- Press logos strip
- Refund policy z link
- Detailed pricing structure

## Persona 3: Industry Press / Reviewer (28-50)

- **Context**: Tech journalists, niche bloggers, YouTubers covering category
- **Needs**:
  - Quick product summary
  - High-res images (press kit)
  - Founder availability for interview
  - Embargo info / launch date
  - Sample/preview unit access
- **Decision speed**: Fast (covering many launches)

**Copy strategy**:
- "Press kit" link visible (footer)
- Founder contact email
- One-pager press summary download
- High-res images downloadable

## Persona 4: Niche Enthusiast (20-50)

- **Context**: Cares deeply about specific category (audiophile, photographer, gamer, etc.)
- **State**: Knowledgeable, opinionated
- **Decision speed**: Medium — researches comparisons
- **Needs**:
  - Detailed specs
  - Comparison vs alternatives
  - Community engagement (Discord / forum)
  - Beta access opportunity
- **Trust signals**: technical depth, community feedback, expert reviewers

**Copy strategy**:
- Spec section comprehensive
- Comparison table
- Community link (Discord, Reddit, Twitter)
- Technical FAQ

## Persona 5: Pre-order Hesitant (25-55)

- **Context**: Interested but unsure if to commit money pre-launch
- **State**: 50/50, on fence
- **Decision speed**: Variable
- **Needs**:
  - "Try before buy" alternative (free waitlist vs paid pre-order)
  - Payment policy clear (kiedy karta obciążona?)
  - Refund policy
  - "Notify when ready" alternative
- **Trust signals**: similar successful launches, no-commitment alternative

**Copy strategy**:
- Two-tier CTA: free waitlist + paid pre-order
- Payment policy explicit ("Karta obciążona przy wysyłce, NIE teraz")
- Refund window mentioned

## Signal detection w brief

| Brief contains | Likely primary persona |
|---|---|
| "early access", "exclusive", "waitlist" | Persona 1 (Early Adopter) |
| "pre-order", "Kickstarter", "Indiegogo" | Persona 2 (Skeptical Buyer) |
| "consumer hardware", "physical product" | Persona 2 + Persona 5 |
| "audiophile", "photographer", "gamer", "niche" | Persona 4 (Enthusiast) |
| "press launch", "review unit" | Persona 3 (Press) |

## Per-persona emphasis

| Element | Early Adopter | Skeptical | Press | Enthusiast | Hesitant |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Hero CTA** | Email field single | Email field + "Learn more" | "Press kit" link | "View specs" | Dual: free waitlist + pre-order |
| **Story length** | Short hook | Detailed founder bio | Press summary | Technical origin | Trust-building story |
| **Specs depth** | Bullets | Comprehensive | High-level | Deep technical | Comparison-focused |
| **Incentive** | Discount visible | Refund policy | N/A | Beta access | "No commitment now" |
| **Risk disclosure** | Light | Heavy | N/A | Medium | Medium-heavy |
| **Counter** | Visible | Optional | N/A | Visible | Optional |

## Launch-type-specific

### Waitlist-only launch
- Best for: Early Adopter primary
- Single-field email form
- Counter prominent
- Discount incentive ("pierwsi X")

### Pre-order launch (paid commitment)
- Best for: Skeptical + Niche Enthusiast
- Risk disclosure heavy
- Refund policy prominent
- Payment timing explicit
- Possibly: 2-tier (free waitlist + paid pre-order)

### Crowdfunding companion
- Best for: All personas
- Link do Kickstarter / Indiegogo prominent
- "% funded" counter
- Stretch goals visible

### Coming soon (no purchase)
- Best for: Press + Early Adopter
- Press kit accessible
- Email capture for "Notify me"
- Build mailing list dla launch announcement

## Polish-specific

### Polish startup launch culture
- Spider's Web, Antyweb, Bezprzewodowy — key tech press
- IndieDB / Wykop tech — community spaces
- Telegram channels (Polish startup founders network)
- Twitter Polish startup hashtags (#polskistartup)

### Currency expectations
- Polish audience: PLN default
- International product targeting Polish + EU: dual PLN/EUR
- US/global product: USD default

### Discount preferences
- "30% off" preferred over "Save 100 zł" (percentage more impactful psychologically)
- "Pierwsi 100" / "Pierwsi 500" — scarcity tactic works
- "Tylko dziś" / "Tylko ten weekend" — urgency tactic
