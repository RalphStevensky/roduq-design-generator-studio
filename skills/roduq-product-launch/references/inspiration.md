- **Co kopiować**: hero shot product + tagline + scroll experience.

### [Robinhood waitlist launch](https://robinhood.com)

## Layout patterns

### Hero
- **Countdown teaser**: timer + product hint
- **Story hero**: provocative statement + product reveal below
- **Video background**: looping product demo (no audio default)
- **Product hero shot**: clean photography + tagline

### Story section
- **Founders' letter**: 3-5 paragraph signed letter
- **Problem → vision**: chronological narrative
- **Behind the scenes**: process / making-of photos

### Waitlist
- **Single field form**: just email — highest conversion
- **Stepped form**: email + 2-3 qualifying questions (segments audience)
- **Social referral**: share to move up list (Robinhood model)
- **Counter visible**: "X osób na liście" / "X% wypełnione"

### Features showcase
- **Spec cards**: 3-5 features z mockups + descriptions
- **Visual comparison**: vs alternatives
- **Iteration timeline**: "wersja 1 → 2 → 3" jeśli product evolved

## Anti-patterns

❌ **"Coming soon" bez date** — feels vaporware
❌ **Long signup form** — kills conversion
❌ **No incentive dla waitlist** — why join?
❌ **No story** — feature list only feels corporate
❌ **Multiple CTAs competing** — single waitlist focus
❌ **Stock product mockups** — looks fake
✅ **Specific date / quarter visible**
✅ **Email-only field** (single field convert higher)
✅ **Counter or scarcity signal** ("pierwsi 500", "324 zapisanych")
✅ **Founders' letter or origin story**
✅ **Real product photos** (even early prototypes — credibility)
