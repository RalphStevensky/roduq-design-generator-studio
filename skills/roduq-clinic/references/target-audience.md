# Clinic — Target Audience Profiles

## Persona 1: First-time Mental Health Patient (22-40)

- **Context**: Has been considering therapy for months/years, finally landing on a website
- **State**: Anxious, hopeful, may abandon if friction too high
- **Decision speed**: Fast (within session) if everything checks out — or instant bounce
- **Needs**:
  - Doctor photo (trust signal #1)
  - Clear "what to expect first visit"
  - Pricing transparent
  - Booking that doesn't require phone call
  - Confidentiality reassurance
- **Trust signals**: practitioner credentials, years experience, philosophy alignment
- **Mobile usage**: high (~70% mobile bookings)

**Copy strategy**:
- Empathetic hero ("Pierwsza wizyta wymaga odwagi")
- "Co Cię czeka" section prominent
- Online booking (Calendly / form) — no phone required
- Pricing visible
- Anonymous testimonials z age range

## Persona 2: Returning Patient / Referral (any age)

- **Context**: Recommended by doctor/friend, or returning after gap
- **Needs**:
  - Quick booking
  - Practitioner availability
  - Same-day appointment option (gdy applicable)
- **Trust signals**: Already trusts practitioner, needs logistical info
- **Mobile usage**: medium

**Copy strategy**:
- Booking CTA prominent (returning users skip context)
- "Same week appointments" / "Następne wolne terminy: data" jeśli applicable
- Contact phone visible (for quick questions)

## Persona 3: Concerned Parent (35-55)

- **Use case**: Seeking specialist dla dziecka (pediatrician, child psychologist, child dentist, child speech therapist)
- **State**: Worried about child, researching multiple options
- **Decision speed**: Slow — 1-2 weeks research, asks pediatrician opinion
- **Needs**:
  - Specialization in children explicit
  - Photos of practitioner working z dziećmi (NIE z dorosłymi)
  - Specialty depth ("Specjalizacja w autyzmie" / "Wczesna interwencja")
  - Insurance info / NFZ accept
- **Trust signals**: child-specific certifications, child-friendly environment photos

**Copy strategy**:
- Hero focus: "{Specialty} dla dzieci i nastolatków" (explicit)
- Services include child-specific descriptions
- Photos of child-friendly waiting room / consultation room
- Approach paragraph: "Współpracuję z rodzicami i dzieckiem"

## Persona 4: Older Adult Patient (60-80)

- **Context**: Long-term health issue, traditional medical practitioner search
- **Decision speed**: Slow, prefers phone consultation before booking
- **Needs**:
  - Phone number prominent (tap-to-call mobile)
  - Address + parking + accessibility
  - Practitioner photo (trust signal)
  - Years of experience prominent
  - NFZ accept disclosure
- **Trust signals**: Years practicing, traditional credentials, local clinic
- **Mobile usage**: low (50% desktop)

**Copy strategy**:
- Phone number w header (clickable mobile)
- Address + parking explicit
- Years experience as large stat
- "Pan / Pani" honorifics
- Larger font sizes (accessibility)

## Persona 5: Health-conscious Self-improver (28-45)

- **Use case**: Dietetyk / fitness coach / preventive health
- **Context**: Not sick, optimizing
- **Decision speed**: Medium (1-2 weeks)
- **Needs**:
  - Concrete methodology
  - Evidence-based language
  - Result tracking system
  - Online consultation availability
- **Trust signals**: scientific credentials, before/after data (NIE photos), reading list

**Copy strategy**:
- Voice: scientific, no-fluff ("Bez diet-cudów. Z dowodami naukowymi.")
- Methodology section detail
- Tracking/measurement system explained
- Online consultations primary (post-COVID norm)

## Polish-specific considerations

### Insurance + NFZ disclosure
- Polish patients ALWAYS ask: "Czy NFZ?" first
- Most private specialists: explicit "Tylko prywatnie" w hero / services
- Some: "NFZ + prywatnie" — różne fees / terminy
- Mental health: usually private only (NFZ limits)

### Discreet entrance dla mental health
- Many Polish therapists w camouflaged office buildings (not standalone clinics)
- Important to mention "dyskretne wejście" / "osobna klatka schodowa" gdy applicable
- Privacy is significant trust signal

### Online vs in-person (post-COVID)
- Mental health: ~60% Polish patients prefer online (data 2024-2025)
- Older patients (60+): prefer in-person
- Dietetyk: ~80% online post-COVID
- Make both options visible

### Polish award/certification visibility
- PTP — Polskie Towarzystwo Psychologiczne (psychology)
- PTPB — Polskie Towarzystwo Psychoterapii Poznawczo-Behawioralnej (CBT therapy)
- IPP — Instytut Psychiatrii i Psychologii (mental health)
- NRL — Naczelna Rada Lekarska (general medicine)
- PTL — Polskie Towarzystwo Lekarskie (general medicine)
- Logos + memberships strip = trust
