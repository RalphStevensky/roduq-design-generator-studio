# Clinic / Medical Practice — Industry References

## Gold standard examples

### [One Medical](https://onemedical.com) (US)
- Calm, modern, trust-building. Photo of provider w warm context.
- **Co kopiować**: clean booking flow, services clearly defined, "what to expect" detail.

### [Headspace Health (Ginger)](https://www.ginger.io)
- Mental health digital-first. Empathetic copy. No clinical jargon w hero.
- **Co kopiować**: language tone for mental health (warm, accessible).

### [Maven Clinic](https://www.mavenclinic.com)
- Women's + family health. Inclusive imagery. Specialist matching system.

### [Better Help](https://www.betterhelp.com)
- Therapy-on-demand. Pattern: testimonials anonymous (initial + age), no photos.

## Polish market specific

### [ZnanyLekarz](https://www.znanylekarz.pl)
- Booking system standard in Polish market. Doctor profile patterns canonical.
- **Co kopiować**: doctor bio format (rating + specialties + tags + price), prosta ścieżka rezerwacji.

### [Telemedi](https://telemedi.com)
- Polish telemedicine. Calm trust voice, polish-language UI.

## Layout patterns

### Hero
- **Doctor photo trust**: portrait of provider w hero (not stock model). Builds immediate trust.
- **Split with credentials**: text positioning + key credentials/years prominent
- ❌ Avoid: stock medical photos (white coat strangers) — credibility killer

### Services
- **List detailed**: full description of each service (mental health = needs context)
- **Cards with icons**: dla many services (dental, dermatology — 8+ procedures)

### Doctor bio
- **Extended bio**: full page w/ education timeline + philosophy + photo gallery
- **Credentials prominent**: hero of about page = diplomas + certifications strip
- Polish convention: "dr n. med." / "mgr" / "specjalista" titles explicit

### Booking
- **Calendly embed**: dla newer practitioners (mental health, English-speaking)
- **ZnanyLekarz button**: Polish standard dla traditional specialists
- **Internal form**: dla practices managing reminders/SMS themselves
- **Phone-only**: NOT recommended (kills millennial bookings)

### Trust credentials
- Diplomas/certificates as image grid (real photos, not generic)
- Professional memberships (PTP, PTL, IPP, NRA — Polish bodies)
- Years of experience as large number
- "Pacjentów rocznie" lub "Lat doświadczenia" as stat

## Anti-patterns

❌ **Stock medical photo (white coat strangers)** — instant credibility killer
❌ **No doctor photo** — patients want to see who they're trusting
❌ **Long clinical jargon w hero** — "Cognitive behavioral therapy for generalized anxiety disorder" → use "Pomoc z lękiem"
❌ **Generic medical icons** (stethoscope/pills/cross) — boring, looks like Bootstrap demo
❌ **Patient photos w testimonials** — RODO + medical ethics violation
❌ **No cancellation policy mentioned** — bookings need this trust signal
✅ **Doctor photo first** — primary trust signal
✅ **Specific years/diplomas/methodology** — concrete > vague
✅ **"Co Cię czeka pierwsza wizyta?"** section — reduces anxiety
✅ **RODO + cookie compliance visible** w footer
