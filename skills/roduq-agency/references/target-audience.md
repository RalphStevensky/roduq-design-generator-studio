# Agency — Target Audience Profiles

> Personas dla `roduq-agency` skill. Voice + content adjustments per buyer profile.

## Persona 1: Mid-Market CMO (40-55)

- **Role**: CMO / VP Marketing / Head of Brand
- **Companies**: 200-2000 employees, B2B SaaS or services
- **Pain points**: Brand inconsistency w marketing materials, in-house team capacity stretched, need partner dla strategic project (rebrand, new website)
- **Decision speed**: 2-4 weeks evaluation (RFP → shortlist → calls → decision)
- **Trust signals needed**:
  - Case studies w similar industry (B2B SaaS dla SaaS client, etc.)
  - Mid-market client logos prominent
  - Founders' biography (who am I trusting?)
  - Process clarity (predictability matters)
- **Budget**: 50-500k PLN za większy projekt, lub 5-30k PLN/mc retainer

**Copy strategy**:
- Voice: confident, work-evidenced, process-clear
- Case studies prominent na hero
- Service grid clean — 3-6 services
- Polish: neutral "Ty/wy" — not "Państwo" (mid-market = less formal niż enterprise)

## Persona 2: Brand Manager / Marketing Manager (30-45)

- **Role**: Brand manager, marketing manager, content manager
- **Companies**: 100-1000 employees
- **Pain points**: Day-to-day execution + need agency dla specific projects (campaign, asset library, photoshoot)
- **Decision speed**: 1-2 weeks for tactical engagement, 3-4 weeks for retainer
- **Trust signals needed**:
  - Service detail clarity (what's included, what's not)
  - Pricing transparency (ballpark even if custom)
  - Speed/responsiveness signals
  - Existing client testimonials z relatable role

**Copy strategy**:
- Voice: practical, fast-turnaround, partner-mindset
- Service detail pages z scope examples
- Case studies w smaller/tactical projects (campaign launch, social asset library)
- "How fast" implied through process timeline

## Persona 3: Startup Founder / Marketing Lead (25-40)

- **Role**: Founder / first marketing hire
- **Companies**: Seed / Series A / B SaaS
- **Pain points**: Need brand foundation + website + first marketing assets, NIE mają in-house marketing team yet
- **Decision speed**: Fast (1-2 weeks) gdy budget allows
- **Trust signals needed**:
  - Startup clients w portfolio (YC / startup logos)
  - Founder-led pricing (50% discount first project, equity option, etc.)
  - Speed signals (initial deliverable w 4-6 weeks)
  - Founder accessibility (direct Slack channel, not account manager)
- **Budget**: 20-100k PLN typical

**Copy strategy**:
- Voice: casual, ambitious, partner-not-vendor
- Service focus: brand foundations + website + design system
- Case studies: other startups grown to Series A/B
- CTA: direct founder calendly link

## Persona 4: Enterprise Procurement (45-60)

- **Role**: Head of Marketing Procurement / Strategic Sourcing
- **Companies**: 5000+ employees, Fortune 500 / large corp
- **Pain points**: Need agency for specific RFP, compliance requirements, vendor diversity
- **Decision speed**: 2-4 months (RFP → shortlist → presentations → committee)
- **Trust signals needed**:
  - Enterprise client logos (Fortune 500)
  - Certifications: ISO 9001, security (SOC 2 if dev involved)
  - GDPR / RODO compliance language
  - DEI / sustainability commitments
  - Geographic coverage / team size depth
- **Budget**: 200k-2M+ PLN

**Copy strategy**:
- Voice: formal Polish ("Państwo"), enterprise-grade, certification-heavy
- "Compliance" section prominent
- Case studies: F500 / blue chip
- Team page: depth + expertise diversity
- Contact: dedicated enterprise inquiry form

## Signal detection w brief

| Brief contains | Likely persona |
|---|---|
| "rebrand", "brand strategy", "identity refresh" | Persona 1 (Mid-Market CMO) |
| "campaign", "social assets", "content production" | Persona 2 (Brand Manager) |
| "startup", "MVP", "first website", "Series A" | Persona 3 (Startup Founder) |
| "enterprise", "Fortune 500", "RFP", "compliance" | Persona 4 (Enterprise Procurement) |
| "global", "multi-market", "localization" | Persona 4 amplified |

## Per-persona content emphasis

| Element | Mid-Market CMO | Brand Manager | Startup Founder | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Hero positioning** | Outcome-focused | Service-clarity | Speed + ambition | Trust + certifications |
| **Case studies** | B2B SaaS / similar industry | Tactical projects | YC / Series A founders | F500 / blue chip |
| **Team page depth** | Founders + key creatives | Service leads | Founder direct contact | Full org + depth |
| **Process emphasis** | Predictability | Speed | Flexibility | Compliance + governance |
| **Pricing transparency** | Ballpark ranges | Service-level | Startup-friendly options | RFP-driven |
| **CTAs** | "Talk to us" + brief form | "See similar work" + email | "Founders' Calendly" | "Enterprise inquiry" form |

## Polish-specific voice notes

- Polish agencies often skip formal "Państwo" w mid-market positioning — feels stiff
- Localize award references: KTR, Effie Polska, ABBY
- Polish press: Marketing przy Kawie, Brief, Press
- IAB Polska membership = trust signal dla advertising agencies
- Currency: PLN primary, EUR for CEE expansion positioning
