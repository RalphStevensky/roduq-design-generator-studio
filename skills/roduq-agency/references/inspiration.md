# Agency Website — Industry References

> Curated dla `roduq-agency` skill.

## Gold standard examples

### [Pentagram](https://www.pentagram.com)
- **Co kopiować**: Work-led layout (case studies hero). Project filtering by partner. Massive image displays.
- **Pattern**: hero = featured project, scroll = grid of all projects.

### [&Walsh](https://andwalsh.com)
- **Co kopiować**: Bold typography statement hero. Color block sections. Process page visualization.
- **Aesthetic**: brutalist (basis dla preset).

### [Instrument](https://instrument.com)
- **Co kopiować**: Service offerings clean grid. Team bio formatting (role + key project link).
- **Tone**: confident, work-evidenced.

### [Huge](https://www.hugeinc.com)
- **Co kopiować**: Case study detail pages — narrative + metrics + visuals interleaved.
- **CTAs**: subtle "Get in touch" w persistent header.

### [Wieden+Kennedy](https://www.wk.com)
- **Co kopiować**: Personality w copy ("We're a creatively driven, independent advertising agency").
- **Voice**: confident-but-human.

## Layout patterns

### Hero
- **Statement text**: large headline (60-80px) z positioning. NIE używaj hero image gdy positioning jest mocna.
- **Split with work**: text left, latest case study tile right.
- **Reel video**: 30s autoplay loop (no audio default) z showreel.

### Case studies
- **Featured 3**: top of homepage, full-width or 3-col equal
- **Masonry**: dla agencies z 10+ projects, asymmetric grid
- **Carousel**: only gdy >6 prac, with swipe affordances

### Services
- **Grid 3-col**: dla agency z 3-6 services
- **Cards with icons**: dla agency oferujących many sub-services
- **Pricing transparency**: skip explicit pricing — agency = custom quotes

## Anti-patterns

❌ **Stock photography placeholders** dla case studies — credibility killer
❌ **Generic agency pageantry** ("Award-winning creative agency delivering bespoke solutions") — boring
✅ **Work + numbers + tags** w case study cards = scannable + credible
✅ **Process section** — dla risk-averse mid-market clients, predictability sells
