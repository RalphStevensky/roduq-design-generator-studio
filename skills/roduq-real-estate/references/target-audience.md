# Real Estate — Target Audience Profiles

## Persona 1: First-time Buyer (28-38)

- **Context**: First property purchase, often dla rodziny
- **State**: Anxious about commitment, learning the process
- **Decision speed**: Long — 6-12 months research, 4-12 weeks active search
- **Needs**:
  - Mortgage calculator
  - "Co musisz wiedzieć kupując pierwsze mieszkanie" guide
  - Cost breakdown transparent
  - Agent who guides patiently
- **Trust signals**: agent rating, sold history, time-with-agency
- **Mobile usage**: high (browsing on phone)

**Copy strategy**:
- Educational content prominent (guides + FAQ)
- Cost transparency upfront
- Agent bios emphasize "patient, explains everything"
- WhatsApp / phone direct

## Persona 2: Move-up Buyer (35-50)

- **Context**: Selling current property + buying larger/better location
- **Decision speed**: Medium — 3-6 months
- **Needs**:
  - Combined sale + purchase coordination
  - Bridge financing info
  - Premium photos of next-tier properties
  - Agent who handles both transactions
- **Trust signals**: experience z move-up cases, smooth coordination

**Copy strategy**:
- "Sprzedaż + zakup" combined service
- Premium listings w hero
- Agent specialization "move-up clients"

## Persona 3: Investor (35-65)

- **Context**: Buying for rental income or appreciation
- **Decision speed**: Fast — knows the market, decisions w 1-4 weeks
- **Needs**:
  - ROI calculator
  - Yield estimate per listing
  - Sold history + price/m² trends
  - Neighborhood appreciation data
- **Trust signals**: market insights blog, agent z investor portfolio
- **Mobile usage**: medium

**Copy strategy**:
- Investment-focused section / page
- ROI / yield prominent na listing
- Market insights blog
- Agent profile "investor specialist"

## Persona 4: International Buyer (30-65)

- **Context**: Foreigner buying w Poland — relocation, investment, second home
- **Decision speed**: 2-6 months (visits + remote)
- **Needs**:
  - English-language UI
  - Process explanation (Polish notariat, NIP, residency)
  - Virtual tours
  - Agent who speaks English fluently
- **Trust signals**: English website, foreign client testimonials, international staff
- **Mobile usage**: medium

**Copy strategy**:
- Bilingual site (PL primary, EN equivalent)
- "Buying in Poland — guide for foreigners"
- Agent profiles z language flags
- Virtual tours / video walkthroughs

## Persona 5: Renter (24-35)

- **Context**: Looking for apartment to rent (often young professional)
- **Decision speed**: Fast — 2-4 weeks
- **Needs**:
  - Monthly cost total (czynsz + media + parking)
  - Move-in date clarity
  - Pet policy / utilities included
  - Photos showing condition realistically
- **Trust signals**: prompt response, no scam vibes
- **Mobile usage**: very high

**Copy strategy**:
- Rental filters prominent
- Monthly total visible (not just rent)
- Direct WhatsApp contact
- Mobile-optimized listing UI

## Polish-specific considerations

### Polish neighborhoods nomenclature
Important to spell correctly + include diacritics:
- Kraków: Stare Miasto, Kazimierz, Podgórze, Krowodrza, Bronowice, Prądnik, Łagiewniki, Nowa Huta
- Warszawa: Mokotów, Powiśle, Stare Miasto, Śródmieście, Wola, Praga Północ / Południe, Ochota, Bemowo
- Wrocław: Stare Miasto, Krzyki, Krzyki-Partynice, Fabryczna, Psie Pole

### Mortgage market (post-2024)
- "Mieszkanie na Start" — government subsidy program (when active)
- Mortgage rate signals: WIBOR-based vs fixed rate
- Currency consideration: most Polish mortgages w PLN, foreign earners mogą EUR

### Transactional documents
- Akt notarialny — notarial deed (required dla sale)
- Umowa najmu — rental contract
- Zaliczka vs zadatek (deposit vs earnest money — different legal consequences)
- KW — księga wieczysta (land register)
- NIP / PESEL required for foreigner buyers

### Trust signals niche
- "Pośrednik z licencją" (licensed broker) — required by law
- Years w branży
- Member of PFRN (Polska Federacja Rynku Nieruchomości)
- Real estate insurance (OC pośrednika)
