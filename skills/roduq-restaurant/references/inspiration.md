## Layout patterns

### Hero
- **Photo fullscreen**: dish or interior (high quality required)
- **Split**: text + photo half/half (works dla concept clarity)
- Avoid: text-only hero — restaurant = visual industry

### Menu display
- **Grid with photos**: dla casual / Instagram-driven places
- **List classic**: dla fine dining (text-led, prices subtle)
- **Tabbed (Lunch / Dinner / Drinks)**: gdy menus differ

### Reservation
- **Form inline** (preferred dla owned channel)
- **OpenTable / Resy embed** (US-centric, less common w Polish market)
- **Phone CTA** (traditional, dla older audience)
- **WhatsApp embedded** (growing dla casual European places)

## Anti-patterns

❌ **Stock food photography** — credibility killer dla restaurant
❌ **Poor mobile menu UX** — most reservations decisions made mobile
❌ **Long flash intros** — slow + annoying + dated
❌ **Generic hero "Welcome to {restaurant name}"** — wastes prime real estate
✅ **Hours visible** w hero or persistent header — most common landing intent
✅ **Address + Google Maps link** w persistent footer
✅ **Phone number clickable** on mobile (tel: protocol)
