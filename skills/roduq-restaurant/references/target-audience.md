# Restaurant — Target Audience Profiles

## Persona 1: Local Regular (28-65)

- **Visits**: 1-4x/month, lunch or dinner
- **Decision speed**: fast — within 2 minutes on landing
- **Needs**:
  - Hours visible immediately
  - Reservation easy
  - Phone number clickable (older audience)
  - Menu accessible (quick scan)
- **Trust signals**: known location, photos look authentic, Polish-language UI
- **Mobile usage**: ~80% of visits

**Copy strategy**:
- Hours w header
- Reservation CTA prominent
- Menu first or second section
- Polish-first ("Ty / wy" casual, "Państwo" gdy fine dining)

## Persona 2: Special Occasion Diner (25-55)

- **Visits**: anniversaries, dates, business dinners — 4-12x/year
- **Decision speed**: slower — browses multiple restaurants, 5-15 min landing
- **Needs**:
  - "Why this place" — concept clarity
  - Photos of food + interior (Instagram-worthy?)
  - Reservation z special notes field (allergies, occasions)
  - Wine list / drinks (gdy applicable)
- **Trust signals**: chef story, reviews, awards (Michelin / Gault Millau)
- **Desktop usage common**: planning at work, booking on desktop

**Copy strategy**:
- Story section z chef bio
- Gallery prominent
- Wine / drinks menu visible
- "Pair with wine" notes w tasting menu

## Persona 3: International Tourist (25-65)

- **Visits**: 1-3 days in city
- **Decision speed**: fast — Google "best restaurant {neighborhood}"
- **Needs**:
  - English navigation prominent
  - Address + Google Maps link
  - Average price range visible (avoid surprise)
  - Photos (skip language barrier)
  - Open in their timezone (note "8-hour difference")
- **Trust signals**: TripAdvisor / Google reviews link, photos of foreign tourists w gallery

**Copy strategy**:
- Bilingual UI clearly visible (flags w header)
- Address + landmark reference
- Price range hero or about ("$$$ — 200-350zł/person")
- Reservation form English-friendly (English error messages)

## Persona 4: Group / Event Organizer (30-50)

- **Use case**: birthday dinners, company events, wedding rehearsals
- **Group size**: 6-30 people
- **Needs**:
  - Capacity confirmation
  - Private dining options
  - Group menu (set menu for X PLN/person)
  - Direct contact for custom requests
- **Trust signals**: photos of private dining areas, previous event mentions

**Copy strategy**:
- "Events" or "Eventy" page link
- Capacity info clear (e.g. "Główna sala: 60 osób · Sala prywatna: 12 osób")
- Phone contact prominent for groups
- Sample group menus z prices

## Per-persona emphasis

| Element | Regular | Special Occasion | Tourist | Event Organizer |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Hours visibility** | Critical | Medium | High | Medium |
| **Menu detail** | Quick scan | Detailed | Photos-heavy | Set menus visible |
| **Reservation** | Phone + form | Form z notes | Form bilingual | Phone direct |
| **Photos emphasis** | Low | High (interior + food) | Very high | Private dining shots |
| **Language** | Polish only OK | PL/EN both | EN-friendly required | Polish for events page |
| **Pricing** | Implicit | Range visible | Range explicit | Group rates clear |

## Polish-specific voice considerations

### Mobile-first reality
- 80%+ Polish restaurant lookups are mobile
- Tap-to-call critical
- Address tap-to-navigate (Google Maps)
- Reservation form mobile-optimized (large tap targets)

### Tourist areas
- Kraków Old Town, Wrocław Market, Gdańsk Long Market: high EN traffic
- Warszawa: business district = EN-heavy lunch hours
- Smaller cities (Lublin, Toruń): polish-only OK

### Reservation behaviors
- Polish guests trust SMS confirmations
- "Bez karty kredytowej" (no card hold) preferred over US-style hold-on-reservation
- Cancellation flexibility valued (up to 2h before)
